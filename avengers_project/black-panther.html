BLACK PANTHER

Black Panther wasn't just a culturally-important film but was also instrumental in expanding the world of the MCU, veering away from the often-American-set stories and introducing completely new characters and a powerful narrative.

Despite its apparent separation from many other MCU projects, the events of Black Panther informed one of the climactic moments of Avengers: Infinity War, which showed the entire Wakandan army go toe-to-toe with Thanos' goons.

Wakanda was even the setting for Thanos' infamous snap, putting it on the map perhaps for all the wrong reasons. Black Panther has made not only Infinity War but also the entire MCU stronger and more significant, a legacy that the upcoming Black Panther: Wakanda Forever will surely carry forward.
